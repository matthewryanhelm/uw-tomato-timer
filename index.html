üìö About the Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It helps boost productivity by breaking work into focused intervals with regular breaks.

  • Step 1: Choose a task to work on
  • Step 2: Set the timer for 25 minutes (one Pomodoro)
  • Step 3: Work on the task until the timer rings
  • Step 4: Take a short 5-minute break
  • Step 5: After 4 Pomodoros, take a longer 15-minute break
